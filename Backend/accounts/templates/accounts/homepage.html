FAQ

You have inquiries. We have the answers.

How can I create animations in the library?

To simply toggle through the library list till you find an animation you are interested in. Click to preview a demo then click the "copy" button at the bottom. You can now paste the class name in your target element's class list

Can I create my own specific animations in the library?

No, you can't. You can, however, create specific animations with the library after download. See documentation page for guide.

Can I save and download animations for further purposes?

Yes, you can! Simply click the love or download button in the library page to either save or download respectively.

Will I be able to access all animations on the offline download?

Of course. Give it a try!
